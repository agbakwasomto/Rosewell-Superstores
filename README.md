# Rosewell-Superstores
Rosewell Superstore Performance Dashboard
# Description
This dashboard provides a comprehensive, interactive overview of business performance for the Rosewell Superstore. It is designed to track key financial metrics, monitor product-level performance, and uncover sales trends over time. The analysis is based on historical transaction data collected between February 2020 and December 2023.
# Data Cleaning
The raw dataset was rigorously pre-processed and cleaned within Microsoft Excel to ensure accuracy and reliability. Power Query was utilized as the primary ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tool to handle the data preparation. This involved standardizing data formats, addressing missing or inconsistent values, creating necessary calculated columns, and structuring the raw data into a clean, tabular format optimized for analysis.
# Data Analysis and Visualisation
The analytical engine of this dashboard heavily relies on Excel's Pivot Table functionality. Pivot Tables were used to aggregate, summarize, and slice the multidimensional dataset, feeding directly into the dynamic visualizations.
The dashboard is divided into intuitive sections and features the following visualizations:
High-Level KPIs: Quick-glance cards displaying Total Products, Total Revenue ($3.36M), Total Profit ($882K), Total Quantity (470k+ Kg), and Total Profit Margin (26%).
Category Breakdown: Bar charts illustrating Revenue, Profit, and Profit Margin distributed across product categories (e.g., Vegetables, Capsicum, Edible Mushrooms).
Time Series Tracking: Line charts mapping Revenue and Profit trends across the months of the year to identify seasonality.
Product-Level Metrics: Donut charts isolating the Top 3 and Bottom 3 products by both Revenue and Profit. Additional bar charts track the Top 5 Product Returns and the Top 10 Products by Loss Rate.
Temporal Heatmap: A detailed matrix highlighting Hourly Revenue split by the days of the week, allowing for peak-time analysis.
Interactive Filters: Slicers on the right-hand panel allow users to dynamically filter the entire dashboard by Year (2020-2023), Month, Day, Hour, Discount status, and Transaction Type (Sale or Return).
# Inferences
Based on the dashboard's visualizations, several key business insights can be drawn:
Overall Health: The superstore maintains a solid overall profit margin of 26%, successfully converting its $3.36M revenue into over $882K in profit across 251 tracked products.
Category Strengths: While general "Vegetables" generate the highest gross revenue, the "Capsicum" and "Edible Mushroom" categories are highly lucrative, showing strong profit yields and high profit margins relative to their sales volume.
Temporal Trends: The line charts reveal distinct seasonality, with revenues and profits experiencing a noticeable dip around mid-year (June/July) before recovering in the late summer and fall.
Peak Shopping Hours: The hourly revenue heatmap clearly shows that weekends (Saturdays and Sundays) drive the highest sales volume, particularly concentrated between the mid-morning and early afternoon hours (10:00 to 14:00).
